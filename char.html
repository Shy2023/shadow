Charcters:

In this anime most of the important charcters are from shadow garden, so lets discuss the members of shadow garden first

shadow garden consist of 7 members

1.

Shadow

The shadow is Cid kagenou real identity which he hides in general.He is the leader and byfar the storgest member of shadow garden, his strength is that much that even beartix is nothing front of him. He is surely the most overpowered charcter in the series

2.

Alpha

second-in-command of Shadow Garden, as well as a descendant of the Elf Hero Olivier. After being cured of Demon Possession by Shadow, she devotes her life to him and his cause. She was the first recruit to become co-founder along with Shadow, and she made shadow garden the strongest org in just two years.

3.

Beta

Publicly, she is known as Natsume Kafka, the author of numerous original pieces that are actually media from Earth told to her by Shadow, which she become as a renowned talented author from her publishings stories and gain many influences as she uses her connections to gather information for Shadow Garden.

4.

Delta

Delta formerly known as Sarah is the fourth member of Shadow Garden, being one of the original Seven Shadows. As a Beastkin Wolf, she acknowledges only strength, swearing loyalty to Shadow in awe of his overwhelming power.

5.

Epsilon

Epsilon is the fifth member of Shadow Garden, being one of the original Seven Shadows. Publicly, she is known as Shiron, a pianist and composer of numerous original pieces of music that are actually songs from Earth taught to her by Shadow, which she able to win various awards and gain popularity among the aristocratic class as she used her status to gather information.

6.

Gamma

Gamma is the third member of Shadow Garden, being one of the original Seven Shadows. Publicly, she is known as Luna, the director of the Mitsugoshi Company which sells original and innovative products through her company, which are all reverse-engineered products developed using the Shadow's descriptions of goods from Earth.

7.

Zeta

Zeta is the sixth member of Shadow Garden and one of the original Seven Shadows, as well as a descendant of the Legendary "Beastkin Hero" Lili. She is the head of exploration and espionage, also leader of her own secret sub-faction, which she founded due to disagreeing with Alpha's leadership.

8.

Eta

Eta is the seventh member of Shadow Garden, being one of the original Seven Shadows, as well as the person in charge of the organization's research faction. Publicly, she is known as Eta Lloyd Wright, a genius architect whose work won Architecture of the Year, having implemented the architectural knowledge of mathematical and scientific principles taught to her by Shadow.

9.

Rose Oriana

Rose Oriana is the former student council president of the Midgar Royal Spellsword Academy and princess of the Oriana Kingdom. Following a plot by the Cult of Diablos, however, she joins Shadow Garden under the codename No. 666 and is later made her country's new queen.

10.

Alexia Midgar

Alexia Midgar is the second princess of the Midgar Kingdom and the younger sister of Iris Midgar.Alexia is shown as a well-mannered young lady whenever she was in school, but in actuality, she is spoiled and abrasive, looking down on others. Currently, she is a student attending the Midgar Royal Spellsword Academy.

11.

Iris Midgar

Iris Midgar is the first princess of the Midgar Kingdom and the elder sister of Alexia Midgar. She is also the leader of the Crimson Order.Iris is a red-headed beauty with fiery straight hair that reaches down to her back and has red eyes.She takes Alpha as her rival.

12.

Clair kagenou

Claire Kagenō is the older sister of Cid Kagenō and heir to the Kagenō Baron Family. Following the Red Moon Incident, she became the vessel of the Demon Diablos.Claire is a young woman with a tall, slender build, as well as long black hair and red eyes evocative of her brother's depiction.
